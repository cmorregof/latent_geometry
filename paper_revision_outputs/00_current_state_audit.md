## Claims Currently Supported

- The local cached embeddings preserve substantial molecular similarity as measured by normalized HA and HA+NA Hamming distance within subtype.
- Latent Euclidean distance has weak global association with absolute temporal separation.
- Exact HA+NA duplicates are common and were removed for TwoNN and local temporal analyses.
- PCA variance is highly concentrated in a small number of components.
- TwoNN estimates remain in a low single-digit range across tested sample sizes and trims.
- Latent nearest neighbors are much closer in collection time than subtype-matched random pairs after exact HA+NA deduplication.
- The analysis does not generate sequences, optimize mutations, reproduce AntigenLM forecasting figures, or make vaccine recommendations.

## Claims Currently Weak or Unsupported

- The bibliography entry for AntigenLM is explicitly marked as needing verification. The exact paper/checkpoint metadata must be verified before journal submission.
- The manuscript does not currently provide a full citation-backed account of protein language models, sequence representation learning, influenza antigenic drift, antigenic cartography, or intrinsic-dimension limitations.
- The phrase "candidate state space" is plausible but must remain conditional. The repository does not contain prospective forecasting validation, antigenic assay validation, phylogenetic validation, or clade/lineage validation.
- Molecular Hamming similarity is not antigenic similarity. No HI assay data, antigenic cartography distances, vaccine-strain labels, neutralization data, or immune-escape measurements are present.
- The current paper does not test phylogenetic distances, clade labels, Nextstrain clades, lineage classification, or tree-aware metrics.
- Spearman correlation alone is not enough for a comprehensive metric validation. Mantel-style tests, distance correlation, trustworthiness/continuity, nearest-neighbor retrieval, and local distance-quantile analyses are not present.
- HA-only and HA+NA Hamming are compared, but NA-only Hamming is not reported in the current full-data paper.
- Hamming distance is computed on nucleotide strings with a 5% length-tolerance rule and truncation to the shorter length. Protein-aware distances, codon-aware distances, BLOSUM-like substitution distances, and amino-acid translations are not currently evaluated.
- TwoNN is the only intrinsic-dimension estimator in the full-data paper. Levina-Bickel MLE, correlation dimension, DANCo, local PCA dimension, and bootstrap/local-window sensitivity are not present.
- No shuffled-label, random-embedding, permuted-year, random-projection, PCA-whitened, or untrained-baseline controls are present.
- No bootstrap confidence intervals are reported for the current full-data paper beyond seed-to-seed standard deviations for pair sampling and TwoNN sensitivity.
- Sampling bias in GISAID, year/subtype imbalance, geography/host composition, and pandemic-era collection effects are not yet discussed with sufficient specificity.
- The current paper is a geometric audit, not a forecasting, vaccine recommendation, or antigenic prediction paper.
